Pandion closes after burning through $41m funding

  • January 15, 2025
  • commercial, express-parcels, financial, news, operations, other-carrier, Postal, usa, USPS

US-based parcel delivery provider Pandion is closing after just five years as it has run out of money despite gaining more than $41m in funding last April and after talks with investors and potential acquirers “have run their course without a favorable outcome.”
